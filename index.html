Features

Welcome to IMSb, the unofficial subsidiary of IMDb, where you can rate and review songs from old classic to the latest hits. Use our site to find new music and to keep in touch with what your friends, and the world at large are listening to. Some of our numerous features include:

  • Extensive database of over 50,000,000 songs
  • Rate and review songs
  • Add your favorite songs and bands to your watchlist
  • Interact with your friends and share song recommendations
  • See what songs are trending
  • Translation into 17 languages

About Us

Founded in 1992 near the dawn of the Internet, technology pioneers Oliver Diamond and Jimmy McShane quickly deciphered the need for a social media app centered around music, years before social media applications became commonplace throughout the world. While many music apps have come and gone throughout the decades, IMSb has had staying power, and this year will celebrate its 30th anniversary on the web.
